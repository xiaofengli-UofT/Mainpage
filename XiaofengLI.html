Biography

Dr. Xiaofeng earned his Ph.D. degree in rock dynamics from Institute of Rock and Soil Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (also University of Chinese Academy of Sciences) and Monash University , under supervisions of Prof. Haibo Li and Prof. Jian Zhao. he then worked in the same group as a Research Assistant Professor for one year, and did postdoctoral research at PolyU from 2020-2021. Currently, he is a postdoc in Grasselli's Geomechanics Group at the University of Toronto.
